## üí° Best Practices

### Flight Booking
- üìÖ Book 2-3 months in advance for best prices
- üïê Tuesday-Thursday flights are 10-15% cheaper
- ‚ö° Direct flights save time but cost 15-20% more

### Hotel Booking
- üè® Book 2-3 months ahead for 20-30% savings
- üìÖ Midweek stays (Sun-Thu) are 15-25% cheaper
- üîÑ Check multiple booking sites

### Visa Planning
- ‚è∞ Apply 4-6 weeks before travel
- üìã Check passport validity (needs 6+ months)
- üì∏ Keep digital copies of all documents

### Insurance
- üí∞ Buy within 14-21 days of booking for best coverage
- üèÇ Adventure activities require specific coverage
- üíä Declare pre-existing conditions

### Currency
- üí≥ Use credit cards for best exchange rates
- üè¶ Withdraw from ATMs (better than exchanges)

### Season Planning
- üéØ Shoulder season = best value (good weather + lower prices)
- ‚òÄÔ∏è Peak season = best weather but expensive
- üí∞ Low season = cheapest but weather issues

### Group Travel
